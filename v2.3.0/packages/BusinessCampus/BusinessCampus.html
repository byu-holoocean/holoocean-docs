BusinessCampus

The business campus environment is around 200m x 200m in size. There are 3 buildings, all of which have interiors that can be inspected. Objects in the level are semantically labeled and will work with the semantic segmentation camera and semantic lidar sensors.

HoloOcean coordinates differ from coordinates listed within the Unreal Engine editor. The following images include coordinates from HoloOcean for the Business Campus level.
